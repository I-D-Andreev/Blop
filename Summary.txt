How to play:
Chase Blop with your mouse (panda) and make him get the carrots. Each carrot adds you
3 seconds to your timer. The game ends whenever you hit yourself in the borders or
when the time expires.

Levels: Easy - Blop moves diagonally
	Hard - Blop moves complexly - each carrot adds you 2 bonus points

Note: Best score is saved
